Meeting Re-Cap - January 8, 2014

  • meeting re-caps

Hey SITers!

As promised, here are the instructions for signing up for our ListServ:

Email [president@ufsit.org](mailto:president@ufsit.org) with “REQUEST [First] [Last]” in the subject line (i.e. - REQUEST Albert Gator).

And how to fill out an Ethics form for next meeting:

Register for [SIT on GatorConnect](https://ufl.collegiatelink.net/organization/SIT) and the form will be available soon.

Also, check out Kernel Sanders on CTFTime.org as well as try some of our introductory challenges! We do have an IRC and you can find instructions on how to access it here as well as a Facebook where meeting announcements (and photos!) will be posted.

See you all next Wednesday!

Meeting - November 20, 2013 | Movie Night!

  • meeting announcement

Hey SITers!

This Wednesday we will be having more of a social meeting and will be watching WarGames!

WarGames is an older, yet still awesome movie that is about a young man who discovers a back door into a new military computer and decides to play a simple game of “Global Thermonuclear War” that could possibly start World War 3. It’s definitely a must see and a good way to unwind before finals.
